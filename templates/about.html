Getting Started with Share Stuff Is Easy

1

Sign Up for a free member account. Sign Up

2

Join an existing Sharing Group. Or create your own Sharing Group.

3

Have items to lend? Simply add items that you'd like to share. You'll receive a notification if someone wants to borrow an item. You then have the option of accepting or declining the borrow request. See below for member's page examples.

4

Want to borrow an item? Browse your group's items and click the borrow button next to the item. The item's owner will be alerted and will then either accept or decline your request. If they accept, you'll work out the details of the pickup time, date, and location, etc via phone, text, or email. see below for member's page examples
